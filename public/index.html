The Promised Neverland Cast

EMMA

Emma is an 11-year-old girl. She is one of the oldest orphans living in Grace Field House. She has a much more optimistic and positive personality than Norman and Ray. .

RAY

Ray is an 11-year-old boy living in Grace Field House. He is a pragmatic person, he often gets full scores on the daily tests of the orphanage, and is a knowledgeable reader.

NORMAN

Norman is one of the oldest orphans living in Grace Field House. He is an 11-year-old boy. His tests are always full scores. He aimed to escape from the house in cooperation with Emma and Ray.

ISABELLA

Known to the orphanage's children as their "Mom", Isabella is a 31-year-old woman who takes care of everyone there. She has fair-skin, purple eyes, and long dark brown hair tied into a low bun. Isabella was born and raised at Grace Field House where she lived a happy life until her crush, Leslie, was shipped out, which brought her intense grief.

KRONE

Known to the children as "Sister Krone", Krone is a 26-year-old woman who was introduced suddenly to help Isabella run the orphanage and aim to become a mom. She is a tall, muscular, and intimidating woman.

DON

Don is a 10-year-old boy living at Grace Field House. He has dark skin, black eyes, short messy black hair, and a rather tall stature. Don is rowdy, talkative, and fun-loving.

GILDA

Gilda is a 10-year-old girl living at Grace Field House. She has brown eyes, short, straight olive hair styled into a bob, and wears round glasses. Gilda is known to be calm, quiet, and shy, but also highly observant.

NAT

Nat is a 9-year-old boy living at Grace Field House. He has short auburn hair, brown eyes, and a tall nose line. Nat is a jovial and slightly narcissistic boy who enjoys playing songs for his siblings on the piano.

ANNA

Anna is a 9-year-old girl living at Grace Field House. She has blue eyes and long, straight blonde hair tied into twin braids. Anna is a bright and gentle girl who loves her family dearly.
